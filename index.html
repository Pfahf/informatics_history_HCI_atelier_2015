Welcome to the History of Informatics page made by the students of Università della Svizzera Italiana's (USI) Faculty of Informatics.

This website aims to explain different areas in the history of informatics between 1968 and 1991. The website is divided into four categories: Hardware, Software, Internet and Games in order to logically split the history into its main elements.

Please use either the navigation bar above (also available on each page), or the links below to browse the site. If the page you visit has related material, there will be a link to the content allowing for an easier browsing experience and a more logical structure between the different areas.
